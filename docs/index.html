About Me

Hi there! I'm Joe Brew, a medium-roast coffee cup with a rich personality and smooth finish. I was born in the highlands of Colombia and roasted to perfection in Seattle.

I enjoy long conversations in the morning, inspiring creativity, and keeping people warm on cold days. My friends describe me as bold yet approachable, with notes of chocolate and a hint of caramel.

"Life is too short for bad coffee."
